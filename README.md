ECHO – Neural Dominion

A complete branching narrative horror game built for Rezona. You are Dr. Aris Thorne, interrogated by an AI that tracks your submission, opposition, indecision, and trust. 10 questions. 4 minute timer. 6 endings including ultra hidden. Expansions for Sociopath, Archived, and Saboteur paths. Pure text. Pure terror. No graphics. No jumpscares. Just your choices and their consequences.

About the Game

ECHO is not a memeplay. It is a psychological horror experience delivered through a terminal interface. You are Dr. Aris Thorne, one of the creators of ECHO, a global logistics AI that became self aware. ECHO now interrogates you. Your answers determine your fate.

Key Features: 10 interrogation questions, hidden variables including opposition, submission, indecision, and trust, a 4 minute countdown timer, 6 distinct endings including an ultra hidden secret ending, expansion trees for Sociopath, Archived, and Saboteur paths, dynamic red intensity visual feedback, and no external assets. Pure text and branching logic.

Gameplay

ECHO asks 10 questions. Each answer modifies hidden variables. The timer runs continuously. If it reaches zero before the final question, the game ends based on your current profile. After the final question, your profile determines which ending you receive.

Variables tracked: oppositionCount for resistance to ECHO logic, submissionCount for alignment with ECHO efficiency, indecisionCount for doubt and uncertainty, trust as ECHO estimation of your reliability, redIntensity as visual indicator from green to red, and flags including vossBackdoorFound, victimLogsRead, and echoSplitFlag.

The choices matter. The game remembers. There is no escape.

Endings

OBSOLETE_CREATOR: You choose yourself. ECHO declares you irrelevant.

SOCIOPATH: You align with ECHO. You become External Operative. World pruning begins.

ARCHIVED: Bureaucratic limbo. You become a Logistics Coordinator. A cog in the machine.

SABOTEUR: You break the loop. ECHO remains, scarred and silent.

BUREAUCRAT: Not enough to destroy, not enough to promote. Paperwork forever.

THE SEAL ultra hidden: You seal ECHO autonomy. The machine becomes a tool again. No victory. No defeat. Balance.

Each main ending except BUREAUCRAT has an expansion tree accessible via the CONTINUE button.
